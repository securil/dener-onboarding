1

Obtain the BNB you will use for the swap

Est. 30–60 min + verification waitYou need: photo ID · bank account

Goal: obtain BNB (used for the MOL swap) and a small amount of POL (for fees). Setup size — referrer: roughly $750–900 equivalent / referrer + evangelist: roughly $1,100–1,300 equivalent.

Route A — an exchange that lists BNB directly (Binance, Bithumb, etc.)

  1. Open an account and complete identity verification (KYC)
    Photo of your ID plus a linked bank account. Verification can take up to a day or two.
  2. Fund the account, then exchange it for BNB
    Obtain your committed-MOL size plus an extra 5–10% to cover fees.
  3. Also obtain about $10–20 equivalent of POL
    Used for the .dex domain registration and Polygon network fees (gas).

Route B — an exchange without BNB (Upbit, etc.)

  1. Exchange into a fast-transfer coin (XRP, TRX, …) and send it to Binance
  2. Exchange it for BNB on Binance
    POL can also be obtained on your local exchange and withdrawn over the Polygon network.
⚠️ In some jurisdictions (Korea, for example) sending funds from an exchange to a personal wallet such as MetaMask requires registering the destination wallet in advance under Travel Rule requirements. Check your exchange's "personal wallet registration" menu before you need it.
Withdraw in STEP 2, not now. Create the wallet first, get its address, then withdraw. Keep the order.

2

Create a MetaMask wallet & move your coins in

Est. 20–30 minBNB ChainPolygon

Goal: create your own wallet (MetaMask), add the two networks you need (BSC and Polygon), then move your coins over from the exchange.

Install the wallet

  1. Desktop: Chrome menu ⋮ → Extensions → search the Chrome Web Store for MetaMask → confirm the fox icon → Add to Chrome
    Mobile: install the official MetaMask app from your app store (check the publisher!)
  2. Create a new wallet → set a password
  3. Write your 12-word seed phrase on paper and store it safely
    Those 12 words are the key to everything you hold. Never photograph them, never send them in a messenger, never tell anyone. Lose them and there is no recovery.

Add the networks (open both roads)

  1. Tap the network selector at the top of MetaMask → Add network → add BNB Smart Chain and Polygon from the list
    If they are not listed, go to chainlist.org → search → Connect Wallet → Add to add them automatically.

Withdraw from the exchange

  1. Click your account name at the top of MetaMask → copy your address 0x…
  2. On the exchange withdrawal screen, withdraw BNB — the network must be BSC (BEP-20)
  3. Withdraw POL — network Polygon
  4. Send a small test amount first → confirm it arrives → then send the rest
⚠️ Choosing the wrong network can lose your coins permanently. Check the "network" field twice before confirming a withdrawal.

Register a .dex domain on DEXignation

Est. 10–15 minPolygon chain

Goal: own a readable internet name (for example myname.dex) as an NFT, to use instead of a long wallet address. It becomes the basis of your referral link and any evangelist activity.

  1. Go to dexignation.com → Connect wallet (top right)
    On mobile, open the site from the "Browser" tab inside the MetaMask app.
  2. Approve the prompt to switch to Polygon
  3. Type the name you want into the search box → check availability
    This name goes into your referral link, so keep it short and memorable (e.g. roy.dex).
  4. Choose a registration period 1 · 3 · 5 · 10 · 15 years → choose a payment token POL / USDT / USDC
  5. Registration takes two signatures: ① commit signature → ② short wait (front-running protection) → ③ confirm signature
    Do not close the window in between — follow the prompts through the second signature.
  6. When it completes, check that the name appears under your account — it is held in your wallet as an NFT
Watch the expiry: renew before the term ends and the name stays yours. Put the expiry date in your calendar.

Set your primary name & attach the BNB wallet that receives rewards

Est. 10 minPolygon chain

Goal: designate the name you just registered as your wallet's official label (primary name), and record behind it the BNB address where rewards will be settled.

Set the primary name

  1. On DEXignation, open your account → pick the name you just registered from your domains
  2. Set as primary name → sign in MetaMask
    Once set, your wallet address displays as this name, and it is what goes into your referral link (ref=yourname.dex).

Register the BNB receiving wallet

  1. Open Manage records on your domain
  2. Add an address record → choose BNB (BSC) from the chain list
  3. Copy and paste the BNB address that should receive your rewards → sign
    A single wrong character means the rewards never reach you. Do not type it by hand.
  4. Check that it displays correctly on digmol.com → My evangelist dashboard
Why is this needed? All rewards are settled in BNB. The address you register here is the wallet that actually receives them.

Swap BNB → MOL and join the ecosystem (Dener designation)

Est. 10 minBNB Chain20,000,000 MOL ≈ $720 equivalent

Goal: swap BNB for MOL on digmol.com to join the ecosystem, and reach a holding of 20,000,000 MOL to receive the Dener designation. The designation is recorded on-chain and anyone can verify it.

  1. First, find out the .dex name of the evangelist who introduced you
    It is the key to your discount (up to 15%). If you don't know it, ask before you swap.
  2. Go to digmol.com → Connect wallet → switch the network to BSC
  3. On the swap (designation) screen, enter the evangelist's .dex name in the Discount domain (.dex) field
  4. Enter the amount of BNB to put in → check the MOL amount calculated automatically
    The exchange ratio is written into the contract, so there is no haggling and no favouritism.
  5. Sign in MetaMask → MOL arrives in your wallet immediately
  6. When your MOL holding reaches 20,000,000, the designation gauge hits 100% — you are a Dener
What your routine looks like afterwards: share your referral link (app.digmol.com/ref?ref=yourname.dex) → rewards are tallied at 22:00 daily and can be claimed from 23:00 → unclaimed rewards do not expire, they keep accumulating on-chain.
⚠️ The MOL exchange ratio runs on a fixed pre-listing reference. For the exact current ratio, always go by the value shown on the swap screen.

Becoming an Evangelist — optional step

30,000,000 MOL ≈ $1,080 equivalentAdministrator registration required

Goal: become an evangelist, who receives 5% of a participation amount as a reward whenever a participant designates them. This is separate from referrer rewards, and both can apply to the same participation (up to 25% on a first-level participation when you hold both roles).

  1. Swap additional MOL until you hold 30,000,000 (same method as STEP 5; roughly $360 equivalent more)
  2. Ask an administrator to register and activate you as an evangelist — punditcode@gmail.com
    Holding the committed MOL alone does not make you designatable. You must be activated on the administrator page.
  3. On digmol.com, open My evangelist dashboard and set the participant discount rate 0–15% (your own 5% is automatic)
  4. Tell participants: "Put my name (yourname.dex) in the discount field on the swap screen and you get a discount."
    That one sentence is the whole of evangelist activity. Designation is optional, so without telling people, no reward is generated.
Suggested path: start as a referrer (about $720 equivalent), learn how the activity works, then extend to evangelist (about $360 equivalent more). If five people designate you per month, rewards corresponding to that extension accumulate over roughly two months (an example, not a guarantee).

Frequently asked questions

What are rewards paid in?

All in BNB. Only the mining rewards from watching ads are paid in MOL. That is why registering a BNB receiving wallet in STEP 4 matters.

Do rewards disappear if I don't claim them?

No. They keep accumulating on-chain and you can claim them at any time. Tallying closes at 22:00 each day, and claiming opens from 23:00.

Isn't it risky to connect my wallet to a site?

Connecting is like showing a business card — no coins move. Coins only move when you press "Sign", so the only habit you need is to read what you are signing (amount, site address) before you confirm.

What if I don't know my evangelist's .dex name?

Ask the person who introduced you, before you swap. Entering it gives you a discount (up to 15%) and gives them the reward they are due. Without it, there is no discount.

Can I designate myself as my own evangelist?

No, self-designation is not permitted. The reward only arises when another participant designates you.

Is this project trustworthy?

The smart contracts have completed two external audits (Beosin), the code is fully public on GitHub, and the sources are verified on PolygonScan so anyone can inspect them. That said, the pre-listing fixed reference for MOL is not a market price, so please participate only within a range that does not strain you.

How much do I need to prepare?

At referrer level: about $720 equivalent of BNB for the committed MOL + the .dex registration fee (check the site; budget $10–50) + $5–10 of network fees ≈ $750–900 equivalent. Holding the evangelist role as well raises the commitment to about $1,080 equivalent, so roughly $1,100–1,300 equivalent. (Reference rate $0.000036; the value shown on the swap screen takes precedence.)

TermIn plain words
WalletThe vault on your phone or browser that holds your coins. You keep the key (the seed phrase) yourself
MetaMaskThe most widely used wallet app. The fox icon
Seed phraseThe 12 words that restore your wallet. Leaked = everything lost. Keep them on paper only
Chain / networkThe "road" coins travel on. This guide uses two: BNB Chain (the yellow road) and Polygon (the purple road)
BNB / POLThe base coin used for fees and payment on each of those chains
Gas feeThe small toll you pay to use a chain
SignPressing "confirm" in your wallet to approve a transaction — like stamping a seal
Travel RuleA regulation (in Korea and other jurisdictions) requiring wallet addresses to be registered and verified before withdrawing from an exchange to a personal wallet
MOLThe base coin of MolePin. Fixed total supply of 6.94 trillion
DenerAn ecosystem partner designated by holding 20,000,000 MOL — a "guardian of the lode"
Committed MOL (PV)MOL placed to qualify for rewards. The starting point of every reward
ReferrerSomeone who receives rewards based on the participation amounts of members who joined through their link (level 1) and those below them (level 2)
EvangelistThe sponsor a participant designates at the moment of the swap. Receives 5% of the designated participation amount
.dex nameA readable internet name (an NFT) used instead of a wallet address. e.g. roy.dex
